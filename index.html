Thirty percent of our adult life is spent at work, so it is crucial that we work in a position that fits our capabilities and work style we want.

This is why we gathered professionals from both technology and Occupational psychology to bring you the easiest and smartest tool to help you find your occupational horizon.

Year of researches, plenty of math and a huge dataset, wrapped with our easy to use web site is the best companion in your career journey.
